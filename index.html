Social media websites

Social media are web-based communication tools that enable people to interact with each other by sharing and consuming information

Social media encompasses a wide range of websites and apps. Some, like Twitter, specialize in sharing links and short written messages

Others, like Instagram and TikTok, are built to optimize the sharing of photos and videos.
